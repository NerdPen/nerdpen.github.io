About

True Fitness

Best Asian Fitness Brand 2019
Distinction Award for Yoga Classes & Facilities
(for TFX, True Fitness and Yoga Edition) (GHP News, 2019)

One of Asia’s largest
True Group is one of Asia’s largest fitness and wellness groups, which mainly consists of businesses in fitness and yoga.

Regional Presence
Established in late 2004, this Singapore brand currently has 25 clubs in Singapore and Taiwan. True Group’s portfolio consists of three brands: True Fitness, TFX and GFX.

Innovate and adapt
True Group won Best Asian Fitness Brand 2019 and GHP Distinction Award for Yoga Classes & Facilities (for TFX, True Fitness and Yoga Edition) at the inaugural GHP News Fitness and Nutrition Awards 2019.The awards acknowledge True Group’s ability to innovate and adapt to market trends and establish itself in an industry where big international companies with longer legacies tend to dominate.

True Fitness True
Fitness is part of True Group with locations in Taiwan and across Singapore.

With its state-of-the-art fitness facilities including functional and weight
training equipment, personal training and the country’s widest variety of
classes from cycling, yoga, HIIT, strength, cardio, dance and more, True
Fitness aims to make every aspect of fitness accessible for all.
